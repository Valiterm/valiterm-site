<section class="about">
  <h2>Micro Torx</h2>
  <p>
    Micro Torx profiles are used where fasteners are very small and tool precision is critical. They are 
    common in electronics, instrument assembly and controlled environment service work.
  </p>
</section>

<section>
  <h3>Typical size range</h3>
  <p>
    T1 to T8 depending on device type.
  </p>
</section>

<section>
  <h3>Common applications</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Electronics and PCB assembly</li>
    <li>Instrumentation and optical equipment</li>
    <li>Precision manufacturing and repair</li>
  </ul>
</section>
