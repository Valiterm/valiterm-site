<section class="about">
  <h2>External Torx (E-Torx)</h2>
  <p>
    External Torx uses a hexalobular head profile driven by a female socket. It is common in applications where 
    the bolt head forms the drive surface directly.
  </p>
</section>

<section>
  <h3>Typical size range</h3>
  <p>
    E4 to E24 in automotive and machinery assembly.
  </p>
</section>

<section>
  <h3>Common applications</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Automotive engine and suspension hardware</li>
    <li>Industrial machinery and service assemblies</li>
    <li>High-load mechanical joints</li>
  </ul>
</section>
