<section class="about">
  <h2>Security Torx (TR)</h2>
  <p>
    Security Torx adds a center pin inside the recess. This prevents the use of standard Torx drivers and requires 
    TR-specific tools. However, TR tools can typically also drive standard Torx screws.
  </p>
</section>

<section>
  <h3>Typical size range</h3>
  <p>
    TR6 to TR40 in general service and equipment locking environments.
  </p>
</section>

<section>
  <h3>Common applications</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Access-controlled panels and housings</li>
    <li>Public infrastructure and fixtures</li>
    <li>Consumer electronics where tamper-resistance is desired</li>
  </ul>
</section>
