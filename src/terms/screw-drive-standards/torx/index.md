<section class="about">
  <h2>Torx (ISO 10664)</h2>
  <p>
    Torx is a hexalobular internal drive system designed to provide stable torque transfer and reduced cam-out 
    compared to Phillips or Pozidriv. It is widely used in industrial assembly and service environments where 
    predictable tightening is important.
  </p>
</section>
