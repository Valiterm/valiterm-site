<section class="about">
  <h2>Torx Plus (IP)</h2>
  <p>
    Torx Plus modifies the internal lobe shape to increase contact surface and allow higher torque before 
    deformation. Interchangeability with standard Torx is not guaranteed.
  </p>
</section>

<section>
  <h3>Typical size range</h3>
  <p>
    IP5 to IP60 depending on mechanical requirements.
  </p>
</section>

<section>
  <h3>Common applications</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>High-torque mechanical joints</li>
    <li>Automotive powertrain and chassis systems</li>
    <li>Heavy equipment service and rebuild work</li>
  </ul>
</section>
