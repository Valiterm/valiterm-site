<h2>Manual Driver</h2>

<p>
  A manual driver applies torque by hand, without powered assistance. It is used when precision, control,
  or low torque is prioritized.
</p>

<h3>Typical use</h3>
<p>Assembly, adjustment, precision work, electrical work, instrument screws.</p>

<h3>If used incorrectly</h3>
<p>Applying excessive force can strip the fastener or damage small drive profiles.</p>

<hr />

**Terminology**
- SV: "Manuell skruvmejsel"
- PT-BR: "Chave de fenda manual"
