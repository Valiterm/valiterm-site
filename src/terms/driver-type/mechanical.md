<h2>Mechanical Driver (Drill/Driver)</h2>

<p>
  A mechanical driver delivers stable, continuous rotational torque using an electric or pneumatic motor.
</p>

<h3>Typical use</h3>
<p>General assembly, wood screws, sheet metal screws, workshop work.</p>

<h3>If used incorrectly</h3>
<p>Using excessive torque without clutch control can overdrive or shear small fasteners.</p>

<hr />

**Terminology**
- SV: "Maskindriven skruvdragare"
- PT-BR: "Parafusadeira"
