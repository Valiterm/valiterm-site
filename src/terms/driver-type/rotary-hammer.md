<h2>Rotary Hammer / Hammer Drill</h2>

<p>
  A rotary hammer applies axial hammering force while rotating. It is used for drilling concrete, masonry, and stone.
</p>

<h3>Typical use</h3>
<p>Anchors, dowels, wall fixtures, structural mounts.</p>

<h3>If used incorrectly</h3>
<p>Fasteners and bits designed for screw driving will shatter under axial hammer load.</p>

<hr />

**Terminology**
- SV: "Slagborr / Borrhammare"
- PT-BR: "Martelete rompedor"
