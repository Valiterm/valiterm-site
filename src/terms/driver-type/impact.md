<h2>Impact Driver</h2>

<p>
  An impact driver delivers rotational torque combined with rapid internal hammering pulses. This reduces cam–out
  and allows fastening in dense or stubborn material.
</p>

<h3>Typical use</h3>
<p>Heavy-duty screws, construction timber, automotive fasteners.</p>

<h3>If used incorrectly</h3>
<p>Non-impact bits fracture. Use bits classified as <a href="/terms/tool-impact-class/">TC1–TC3</a>.</p>

<hr />

**Terminology**
- SV: "Slagskruvdragare"
- PT-BR: "Parafusadeira de impacto"
