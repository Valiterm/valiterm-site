<section class="about">
  <h2>Impact Rated (TC2)</h2>
  <p>Optimized for continuous impact loads in high-torque drivers where shock energy is transferred directly through the bit.</p>
</section>

<section class="why">
  <h3>When to use</h3>
  <p>Use for structural fastening, framing screws, heavy assembly, and production environments.</p>

  <h3>If misused</h3>
  <p>Using non-impact tools here causes instantaneous chipping, catastrophic fracture, or driver damage.</p>
</section>
