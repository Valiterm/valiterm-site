<section class="about">
  <h2>Light Impact (TC1)</h2>
  <p>Designed for intermittent impact or moderate shock loads, typically found in light portable drivers.</p>
</section>

<section class="why">
  <h3>When to use</h3>
  <p>Use where impact occurs occasionally, such as screw assembly in construction fit-out or mixed tool workflows.</p>

  <h3>If misused</h3>
  <p>Overloading TC1 in continuous impact applications causes torsional fatigue and tip rounding.</p>
</section>
