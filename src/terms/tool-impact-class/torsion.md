<section class="about">
  <h2>Torsion Zone Impact (TC3)</h2>
  <p>Features an engineered torsion section that flexes to absorb shock loads and prevent brittle fracture at the drive end.</p>
</section>

<section class="why">
  <h3>When to use</h3>
  <p>Use in dynamic fastening where impact intensity varies, especially in long-bit or deep-reach driver situations.</p>

  <h3>If misused</h3>
  <p>Using torsion bits in non-impact tasks reduces precision and can introduce over-rotation or soft engagement feel.</p>
</section>

<section class="caption">
  <strong>Terminology (EN / SV / PT-BR)</strong><br>
  Torsion Zone • Torsionszon • Zona de torção
</section>
