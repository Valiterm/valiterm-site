<section class="about">
  <h2>Non-Impact Rated (TC0)</h2>
  <p>Designed for rotational torque only. These bits and drivers are intended for controlled fastening where no shock or impact loads are present.</p>
</section>

<section class="why">
  <h3>When to use</h3>
  <p>Use in precision assembly, electronics, cabinetry, and general manual fastening where load is smooth and predictable.</p>

  <h3>If misused</h3>
  <p>Using TC0 bits in shock or impact environments leads to cam-out, edge shearing, or premature fracture.</p>
</section>
